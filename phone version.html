Project
Samskriti

Envisioned by Sadhguru, Project Samskriti is a contemporary ensemble performing and offering Indian classical arts across the globe. Seamlessly blending classical dance, music and martial arts into an exuberant expression of art and spirituality, Project Samskriti transcends cultural boundaries and captivates audiences of all backgrounds.

The group comprises alumni of Sadhguru Gurukulam Samskriti, a unique school in southern India where children begin training in Indian classical arts at the age of eight. Having imbibed these ancient arts as a part of themselves, they exude the graceful power of Kalaripayattu and embody the perfect balance of Bharatanatyam, making their every movement and expression a delight to behold.

Featured Performance

anumArga

— Seeking a Path —

by Project Samskriti

Devotion & Seeking

The production includes poetry in adoration of Krishna, where devotees journey to Vrindavan celebrating the playful glory of the young Krishna. It also presents Arjuna’s intense sadhana as he seeks Shiva’s blessing for the Pashupatastra, culminating in his encounter with Kirata.

Tapas & Surrender

We witness Parvati’s deep tapas in her unwavering seeking of union with Shiva, and explore the beauty of surrender through Ahista Ahista.

Devotion & Inner Vision

The production also includes the story of the great devotee Ravana, whose unique blend of devotion and arrogance leads him on a quest to Kailash, and the powerful tale of a wandering saint who offers his eyes to reveal the possibility of inner vision.

Shoonya

Finally, through abstract movement, the production explores Shoonya — the movement of breath and the creation of the universe, reflecting on the nature of existence and the emptiness from which life emerges.
